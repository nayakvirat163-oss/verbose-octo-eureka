About Us

Our Mission

Chinmaya Vidyalaya, Bokaro is committed to providing holistic education that nurtures intellectual, emotional, and spiritual growth. We believe in creating future leaders who are not only academically excellent but also morally strong.

Our Vision

To be a center of educational excellence where students develop critical thinking, creativity, and compassion. We aim to build character, instill values, and prepare students for global citizenship.

Key Highlights

  • CBSE Affiliated School
  • Established in 1999 with 25+ years of excellence
  • 2800+ students across classes 3 to 9
  • Highly qualified and experienced faculty
  • State-of-the-art infrastructure
  • Focus on values and character building

Eligibility & Required Documents

Eligibility Criteria:

  • • Class 3: Age 7-8 years (as on 31st March 2025)
  • • Class 4-9: Previous class mark sheet required
  • • Transfer Certificate from previous school
  • • Character Certificate

Required Documents:

  • • Birth Certificate (original + photocopy)
  • • Aadhar Card of student & parents
  • • Latest passport size photographs (4 copies)
  • • Address proof (electricity bill/rent agreement)
  • • Previous year mark sheet & TC
